Press Alt+F4 to close the demo.  

Keyboard & Mouse Controls:  
    &emsp;- W = Throttle  
    &emsp;- S = Brake  
    &emsp;- A = Steer Left  
    &emsp;- D = Steer Right  
    &emsp;- Space = Handbrake  
    &emsp;- Mouse = Move Camera  
    &emsp;- Left Shift = Pitch Up (while airborne)  
    &emsp;- Left Ctrl = Pitch Down (while airborne)  
        
 Gamepad Controls:  
    &emsp;- Right Trigger = Throttle  
    &emsp;- Left Trigger = Brake  
    &emsp;- Left Stick = Steer (and pitch while airborne)  
    &emsp;- Right Bumper = Handbrake  
    &emsp;- Right Stick = Move Camera  

Note: if car is upside down you can steer left or right to flip it over.
